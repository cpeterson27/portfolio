# Notion Builder OS

Notion Builder OS is a focused product workspace for solo builders and early founders. It is designed around one repeatable loop:

**Capture → Decide → Ship → Learn**

This folder contains the source files for building the customer-facing Notion template.

## Included

- Six import-ready CSV databases with realistic example records
- Page templates for product briefs, experiments, decisions, weekly shipping, launches, and proof
- A dashboard layout blueprint
- A complete Notion assembly guide
- A product QA checklist
- Customer quick-start and welcome copy

## Product promise

Move a product idea from rough notes to a clear decision, a weekly shipping plan, a launch checklist, and reusable proof without building an oversized “second brain.”

## Database map

1. Products — command center and master product record
2. Ideas — opportunity and idea pipeline
3. Decisions — decision history and rationale
4. Experiments — assumptions and smallest useful tests
5. Weekly Shipping — weekly milestones and outcomes
6. Proof Library — customer language, screenshots, results, and case-study evidence

Notion does not create relations or rollups from CSV imports. The setup guide explains the small amount of manual configuration needed after import.
